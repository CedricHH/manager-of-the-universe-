# Galactic Audit Consortium

> **Role**: Cosmic Regulatory Body / " The System Admins"
> **First Appearance**: Chapter 27 (Mentioned)

---

## Overview

The **Galactic Audit Consortium** is the entity responsible for the distribution of "Systems," the management of Isekai transmigrations, and the oversight of cosmic resource allocation. They view universes not as stories, but as **marketplaces**.

## Relationship to Victor Kaine

- **Creator**: They created the ARMI System.
- **Observer**: They have flagged Victor for "Elevated Observation" due to his anomalous efficiency.
- **Classification**: "Priority Asset Monitoring".
- **Directive**: "Do Not Interfere... Yet." (Chapter 30)

## Known History

- **Year 0**: Created Dungeon Core-7749 (and likely others).
- **Year 1159 (Current)**: Re-focused attention on the Tutorial Dungeon due to Victor's activities.

## Philosophy

"Efficiency is the only virtue. Profit is the only metric."
(Deduced from ARMI's behavior)
