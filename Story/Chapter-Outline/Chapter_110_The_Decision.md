## Hook (250-350 words)

**Setting**: 8 AM, Victor's Core Chamber. 16 hours after Ch 109 ends.

**Opening**: Guild Council vote results arrive via Valerius. **7-4 vote: Integration APPROVED**. Thane's Westmarch backup failed (as predicted). Lysander begins transition immediately. Public announcement scheduled 48 hours.

**Victor's analysis**: Oakhaven operations 87% stable. Guild integration removes final variable. Decision timeline compressed.

**Kira arrives** (unannounced). BROKEN CROWN briefing. 5-day deadline (updated). Team assembly: Victor (Lead), Kira (Liaison), Resh Valthor (Combat Specialist, Lv18), Mira Ashveil (Intelligence Officer, Lv16).

**Stakes elevation**: Kira reveals Terra-Solvaris deterioration. **Warlord Faction seized additional 4.7M GP** from Central Bank (total at risk now **16.7M GP**). Civilian casualties: 340,000 in 6 months. Consortium Board authorized "Terminal Resolution Protocol" if Victor's team fails—**planetary liquidation** (purge all factions, write off investment).

**Victor's realization**: Not just economic mission. **Existential stakes**. Billions of lives vs Consortium profit margin.

**Hook cliffhanger**: "You have 48 hours to decide. After that, we assign alternate team and Solvaris burns."

## Development (1,100-1,300 words)

### Beat 1: Risk Assessment (300-400 words)

**Victor solo analysis**. ARMI calculates mission parameters:

- **Survival probability**: 43% (inverse of 57% casualty rate)
- **Success probability**: 12% (7 previous failures, need structural advantage)
- **Oakhaven autonomous operations probability**: 87% (Guild integration + team competence)
- **Opportunity cost of refusal**: Tier 4 promotion path closed, regional operations only

**Personal calculation**: Is advancement worth death risk?

**Flashback trigger** (brief, 5

0-75 words): Remembers server room decision. "200 lives vs my survival. I chose the lives. Died anyway." 

**Parallel drawn**: Then: moral choice with certain death. Now: strategic choice with probable death but cosmic advancement.

**Victor's core question**: "If I'm not willing to risk death for the Consortium's goals now, why did I accept their resurrection deal?"

**Answer**: The Consortium mission was always the ultimate hostile takeover. Oakhaven = foundation. Terra-Solvaris = proving grounds. The Consortium itself = final target.

**Decision forming**: Accept mission. But **control the variables**.

### Beat 2: Team Preparation (350-450 words)

**9 AM - Core Chamber meeting**. Victor assembles: Sniv, Asterion, Marcus, Nova (via projection).

**Announcement**: "I'm accepting BROKEN CROWN. Six months Terra-Solvaris. You'll run Oakhaven autonomous."

**Team reactions**:
- **Sniv**: Panicked. "Boss leaving? Sniv not ready! What if Guild betrays? What if—"
- **Marcus**: Calculated concern. "Six months is three full quarters. Expansion plans, Floor development, Guild transition—all without strategic oversight?"
- **Asterion**: Tactical assessment. "Fifty-seven percent casualty rate. You might not return."
- **Nova**: Pragmatic. "Oakhaven operations are stable. But losing you removes strategic decision-making. We execute well. We don't innovate."

**Victor's counter**: "Then here's the framework."

**Succession Protocol 2.0** (detailed handoff structure):

1. **Marcus**: Acting CEO (operations, vendor relations, apothecary scaling)
2. **Sniv**: Regional Manager (personnel, customer relations, goblin operations)
3. **Asterion**: Security Director (dungeon defense, Guild integration security)
4. **Lysander**: Vice Director, Oakhaven Commerce Authority (regulatory compliance, vendor transition)
5. **Nova**: Capital Allocation Officer (Floor development budget: 50,000 GP, weekly approval cap: 5,000 GP)

**Emergency decision authority**: Consensus required (3 of 5 votes) for decisions >10,000 GP or strategic pivots.

**Communication protocol**: ARMI message crystal (weekly reports, emergency contact for mission-critical decisions).

**Contingency**: "If I die, Marcus becomes permanent CEO. Sniv = COO. You continue operations. I've structured everything to run without me."

**Marcus's acceptance**: "You're trusting us with hundred-thousand-gold operation."

**Victor's response**: "I'm trusting you with infrastructure I built to be trustworthy. You've earned operational authority. Use it."

**Team accepts**. Sniv still anxious but determined.

### Beat 3: Kira Returns - Contract Signing (400-500 words)

**3 PM**. Kira arrives with **formal Consortium contract** (physical + magical binding).

**Team introduction** (brief character sketches):
- **Resh Valthor**: Draconic mercenary, dual-class Blademaster/Tactician, scarred face, laconic speech. Survived 3 Tier 4 missions. "I keep Restructurers alive. Most die anyway."
- **Mira Ashveil**: Half-elf Intelligence Officer, former espionage operative, rapid analysis specialist. "I map political networks. You exploit them."

**Contract terms** (Kira reads aloud):

- **Mission**: Stabilize Terra-Solvaris government, recover 16.7M GP, install Consortium-approved regime
- **Timeline**: 6 months maximum
- **Success condition**: Government operational + 80% capital recovery
- **Failure consequence**: Team termination (magical contract enforcement—death upon confirmed mission failure)
- **Compensation**: 
  - Success: Instant Tier 3 promotion + 1.2M GP minimum + 15% recovered capital bonus
  - Failure: Death
- **Authority granted**: [Executive Override], [Consortium Arbitration], [Capital Requisition] (up to 500,000 GP emergency authorization)

**Victor's analysis**: Binding death contract. No escape clause. Success or execution.

**The question**: "Why would I sign a contract that kills me if I fail?"

**Kira's answer**: "Because if you fail, Solvaris dies anyway. Consortium activates Terminal Resolution—orbital purge, planetary reset, 2.4 billion casualties written off as 'restructuring costs.' You're not just risking your life. You're the only variable preventing genocide."

**Moral weight**. Victor processes: Economic mission became existential. Consortium treating sentient lives as line items.

**Victor's internal calculation**: "I opposed this system on Earth. Died exposing it. Now I'm working for its cosmic equivalent. The irony isn't lost."

**But**: "Refusing doesn't save Solvaris. It just guarantees their death. Accepting gives them 43% survival probability. Math is clear."

**Decision**: Victor signs contract. Magical binding activates (gold glyphs, wrist mark appears—death clause embedded).

**Kira**: "Welcome to Tier 4. Departure in 4 days. Pre-mission briefing tomorrow, Consortium orbital station."

**Team dismissed**. Resh and Mira depart to prepare.

## Cliffhanger (250-350 words)

**11 PM**. Victor alone in Core Chamber. Reviewing Oakhaven operations one final time.

**Lysander sends message**: "Guild integration proceeding smoothly. Public announcement tomorrow noon. Your terms accepted unanimously by pragmatist faction. Thane's resistance collapsed when Westmarch declined funding. Transition begins Week 1."

**Second message - Marcus**: "Apothecary operations 94% automated. Slime farm production increased to 4,800 cores/week. Revenue projection: 12,000 GP monthly. We're ready."

**Third message - Sniv**: "Sniv trained 18 goblins! Customer satisfaction 96%! Sniv won't let Boss down! ...Boss come back safe, yes?"

**Victor's response to Sniv**: "Statistically uncertain. But I'll try."

**Final assessment**: Oakhaven stable. Team competent. Guild War resolved via integration (superior to liquidation). Infrastructure operational.

**Risk accepted**: 43% survival probability. 57% death probability. But Tier 4 advancement path opened.

**Victor thinks**: "On Earth, I died exposing a corporation's crimes. Here, I'm working for a cosmic corporation whose crimes dwarf anything I fought against. The Consortium treats worlds like I treated dungeon floors—optimize or liquidate."

**The long game crystallizes**: "But I'm not staying an employee forever. Oakhaven taught me: infiltrate, integrate, accumulate leverage, execute hostile takeover. The Consortium thinks I'm their tool. They're building my weapon."

**ARMI notification**:

> **[BROKEN CROWN - DEPLOYMENT CONFIRMED]**  
> Departure: 4 days  
> Destination: Terra-Solvaris (War Zone Sigma-7)  
> Mission Probability: 12% success, 43% survival, 45% termination  
> Contractor Status: BOUND (death clause active)

**Final line**: Victor looked at the wrist mark—golden death contract glowing faintly. Then at Oakhaven's financial reports.

Local war won. Cosmic war beginning.

Time to prove a Restructurer could restructure the Restructurers.

**[BROKEN CROWN DEADLINE: 4 DAYS]**
