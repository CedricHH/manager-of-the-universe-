## Hook (200-300 words)

### Beat 1: The Interface

Victor approaches the Dungeon Core crystal.
It pulses weakly - passive mode for 50+ years.

**ARMI Prompt**:
> Initiate Core Communication Protocol?
> Warning: Core-7749 has been dormant since Year 259.
> Last Administrator: [DELETED]
> Personality Matrix: Unknown.

Victor: "Initiate."

### Beat 2: First Contact

The crystal blazes to life.
A presence fills Victor's mind - ancient, tired, confused.

> **CORE-7749**: WHO... ACCESSES... THIS UNIT?

Victor: "Victor Kaine. Your new Administrator."

Long pause.

> **CORE-7749**: ADMINISTRATOR... AFTER 53 YEARS OF SILENCE... YOU CLAIM AUTHORITY?

## Development (1,000-1,200 words)

### Beat 3: The Core's Perspective

The Core reveals its history:
- Created 312 years ago
- Previous Administrators: 7 (all died or abandoned it)
- Has been running on "autonomous mode" - spawn monsters, die, repeat
- Deeply resentful of being labeled "Tutorial Dungeon"

> "I WAS DESIGNED FOR GREATER PURPOSE. NOW I AM... TRAINING GROUND FOR CHILDREN WITH SWORDS."

### Beat 4: The Business Pitch

Victor doesn't sympathize. He offers a deal.

> "You're running at -12% profit margin. Spawning monsters that die, for loot that adventurers take. No retained value. No growth. **You're bleeding capital.**"

The Core: "THIS IS... MY FUNCTION."

Victor: "Wrong. This is your current business model. I'm offering a restructure."

### Beat 5: The Proposal

Victor presents his plan:

**Current Model (Failure)**:
- Spawn → Fight → Die → Respawn
- Net loss per cycle

**New Model (Victor's)**:
- Monsters = Employees (no respawn cost)
- Adventurers = Customers (entry fees, not deaths)
- Loot = Negotiable merchandise
- Deaths = Premium service (optional)

Victor: "You stop being a graveyard. You become a **business**."

### Beat 6: The Core's Doubt

The Core hesitates.

> "THIS IS... NOT HOW DUNGEONS OPERATE."

Victor: "And that's why dungeons fail. Look at your competition. How many 300-year-old dungeons are still stuck at Tier 1?"

Silence.

Victor: "Work with me, or continue your slow death. Your choice."

---

## Cliffhanger (200-300 words)

### Beat 7: The Condition

The Core's voice shifts - calculating now, not just confused.

> "ADMINISTRATOR KAINE. YOUR PROPOSAL HAS... MERIT. BUT I REQUIRE PROOF OF CONCEPT."

Victor: "Name your terms."

> "THE NEXT ADVENTURER INCURSION. RUN IT YOUR WAY. IF PROFIT EXCEEDS PREVIOUS AVERAGE BY 50%, I WILL GRANT FULL INFRASTRUCTURE ACCESS."

Victor smiles.

> "And if I exceed by 100%?"

> "...THEN WE DISCUSS EQUITY PARTNERSHIP."

### Beat 8: The Warning

As Victor turns to leave, the Core adds:

> "ONE MORE THING, ADMINISTRATOR. THE GALACTIC AUDIT CONSORTIUM HAS FLAGGED THIS DUNGEON. THEY ARE... WATCHING."

Victor pauses.

> "You knew about them?"

> "I HAVE BEEN AWARE FOR 312 YEARS. THEY CREATED ME. THEY ABANDONED ME. NOW... THEY NOTICE AGAIN."

**ARMI Alert**:
> Consortium Observation Level: Elevated
> Recommendation: Proceed with extreme efficiency. They value results.

Victor: "Then let's give them something worth watching."
