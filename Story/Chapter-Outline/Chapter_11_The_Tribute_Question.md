## Hook (200-300 words)

### Beat 1: The Emergency Meeting

Victor gathers his "Board" (Sniv + the 4 team leads).
He explains the situation.
> "The Minotaur wants payment. We have 3 days."

### Beat 2: Options Analysis

Victor lists the options:
1. **Pay** (Food/Gold): Lose resources, appear weak
2. **Fight** (Souls): Die (Asterion is Lv 25)
3. **Flee** (Abandon dungeon): Start over with nothing
4. **Negotiate** (???): Risky

The goblins vote: "Run."
Victor shakes his head.
> "We don't run. We find leverage."

**Entertainment. Purpose. A worthy opponent.**

Victor drafts a counter-offer:

## Cliffhanger (200-300 words)

### Beat 7: The Source

Victor remembers: The dead hero's scroll mentioned "Sir Kael of the Silver Dawn."
If heroes have come before, heroes will come again.
Victor doesn't need to feed Asterion his goblins.
He needs to feed him **adventurers**.

### Beat 8: The Risk

To get adventurers, the dungeon needs to be **known**.
That means exposure. That means the outside world.
That means Victor stops hiding.

> "Sniv. I need you to do something dangerous."
> "I need you to leave the dungeon."

Sniv's eyes go wide.
> "The... outside?"
