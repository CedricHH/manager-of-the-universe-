Asterion enters.

It's not a fight to the death.
It's a **performance evaluation**.

## FLASHBACK: Discovery of the Truth (Conclusion)

### The Choice

Victor had everything.

Evidence. Names. Dates. Enough to destroy the board.

He could sell it. Blackmail them. Walk away rich.

Or...

### The USB Drive

He compiled it all onto a single drive.

Then he made copies. Sent them to:
- Three journalists (different outlets)
- The families' lawyers
- A regulatory contact in Geneva

Anonymous. Untraceable.

### The Question

As he finished, his phone buzzed. A message from an unknown number:

> *"You could've sold this. Why didn't you?"*

Victor stared at the message.

He didn't know the answer.

He typed back: *"Ask me when I figure it out."*

The sender: *"I will. -S"*

**Sarah. She'd been watching. She'd sent the original email.**
