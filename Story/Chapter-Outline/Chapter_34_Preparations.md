## Hook (200-300 words)

### Beat 1: The Briefing

Victor assembles his leadership team:

- Nova (Core)
- Asterion (CSO)
- Sniv (COO)
- Krog (Guard Lead)

Victor: "I'll be gone for approximately one week. During this time, Sniv has operational authority. Asterion handles security. Nova monitors everything."

### Beat 2: The Concerns

Sniv: "Boss... what if bad humans come?"

Victor: "Iron Wolves are scheduled for return visit in 4 days. Standard protocol. Charge fees. Let them through. Don't kill unless provoked."

Asterion: "And if someone stronger comes?"

Victor: "Nova will signal me. But our profile is low. No one knows this dungeon matters yet."

## Development (1,000-1,200 words)

### Beat 3: The Disguise

Victor can't walk into Oakhaven looking like a dungeon lord.

He needs a cover.

**ARMI Suggestion**:
> Cover Identity: Traveling Merchant
> Equipment: Civilian clothing, fake trade license
> Companion: Zip (disguised as "exotic pet")

Victor: "Zip, can you pretend to be a pet?"

Zip: "Zip can pretend many things! Zip good actor!"

Victor: "You'll be in a bag. Don't talk."

Zip: "...Zip less good at not talking."

### Beat 4: The Inventory

Victor prepares supplies:
- 50 GP (operating cash)
- Premium dungeon goods (samples for "trade cover")
- R#1's journal (hidden, for reference)
- Black card (most important item)

Nova: "RECOMMEND CAUTION. CONSORTIUM WAYPOINTS MAY BE MONITORED."

Victor: "That's a risk I have to take. I can't stay at Level 1 forever."

### Beat 5: The Goodbye

Moment before departure.

Sniv approaches Victor privately.

> "Boss... you come back, yes?"

Victor pauses. Sniv's loyalty is... uncomfortable.

> "I always protect my investments, Sniv. You're my investment."

Sniv beams. He doesn't understand it's not quite affection.

Or maybe he does, and it's enough.
