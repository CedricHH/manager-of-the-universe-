## Development (1,000-1,200 words)

### Beat 2: FLASHBACK - The Empty Months (1.5 Years Ago)

**Setting**: Victor's apartment. Still expensive. Still empty.

He has the USB drive.
He hasn't opened it yet.
He just... exists.
Wakes up. Stares at walls. Drinks. Sleeps.

### Beat 3: The Media Storm

The TV is always on.
> "Victor Kaine, once a rising star, now facing 15 federal charges..."
> "The 'Chainsaw' has been cut down..."
His old nickname. Earned for restructurings. Now a slur.

### Beat 4: The Visitors

**Day 30**: His lawyer visits.
> "Victor, if you plead guilty, you might get 10 years instead of 25."
Victor doesn't respond.

**Day 60**: His mother calls.
He doesn't answer.

**Day 90**: Elena Chen (His betrayer) sends flowers. With a card:
> "I'm sorry it had to be you. - E"
He burns the flowers.

### Beat 5: The USB Drive

**Day 150**: Victor finally opens the USB.
Contents:
- Internal emails (Board members discussing the cover-up)
- Financial records (The money trail)
- A video (A factory in the Congo. Children. Death.)

**The Truth**: The conflict minerals scandal killed 47 people. Including 12 children.
Victor didn't know. But his signature is on the contracts.

### Beat 6: The Weight

Victor stares at the video.
He was complicit. Unknowingly. But still.
He spent his life optimizing. Cutting costs. Increasing efficiency.
And this is where the efficiency led.

---

## Cliffhanger (200-300 words)

### Beat 7: Back to the Dungeon

Victor opens his eyes.
The dungeon is dark. Quiet.
Sniv is asleep nearby.

Victor looks at his hands.
Different body. Same guilt.

### Beat 8: The Resolve

He stands.
He can't undo the past.
But he can build something different.
Something where efficiency doesn't require bodies.

> "Sniv. Wake up."
> Sniv: "Mmph... Boss?"
> Victor: "New project. I need you to count every trap in this dungeon."
> "We're going to make them... less lethal."
