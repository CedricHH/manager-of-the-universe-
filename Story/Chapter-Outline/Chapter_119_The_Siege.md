**Victor's preparation pays off**: Northern defenses strongest. Kill zones devastating. Revolutionary advance stalls.

**Key combat beats**:

**Complication**: Vex adapts. Shifts forces to eastern approach. False intelligence exposed.

### Beat 2: Eastern Crisis (~400 words)
**Escalation**: Revolutionary mages breach eastern wall section. Infantry pouring through gap.

**Victor's response**: Rapid redeployment. Pulls reserve forces from southern wall, redirects Kieran's mercenaries to plug breach.

**Crisis moment**: Gap widening. Revolutionary forces establishing foothold inside walls.

**Resh deployment**: "Resh. Eastern breach. Full authorization."

**Combat showcase (brief)**: Resh engages. Anti-magic aura disrupts Revolutionary mages. Martial dominance clears breach zone. 67 casualties in 4 minutes.

**Breach sealed**: But Revolutionary forces now know *exactly* where walls are weakest. They're learning.

### Beat 3: Tribune Vex Engages (~400 words)
**Climax**: Vex personally leads second eastern assault. Level 17 Battle Mage with fresh barrier.

**Difference from Ch 113**: Vex has learned. Barrier now dispersed, not concentrated—no single point of failure.

**Victor's analysis**: "He adapted. Can't break barrier the same way."

**Sera's intelligence**: "Dispersed barrier has lower intensity. He can maintain it longer, but physical attacks might penetrate where magical can't."

**New strategy**: "Resh—physical assault. Mass infantry behind him. He breaks the barrier, infantry exploits the gap."

**Execution**: Coalition infantry assault behind Resh's breakthrough. Vex's barrier fragments under physical pressure. Revolutionary command structure disrupted.

**Vex wounded again**—but escapes. Revolutionary assault falters.
