## The Divine Economy

Gods need worship. Worship = power.

Aurum's problem: Her domain is COMMERCE. People pray to war gods, harvest gods, love gods...

Nobody prays to the god of balanced ledgers.

She's been slowly dying for millennia.

---

## Victor's Prosperity Doctrine

When Victor's "Prosperity Doctrine" spreads across the world, something happens:

People start praying for **good deals**. For **fair contracts**. For **profitable ventures**.

Aurum's power EXPLODES.

She seeks Victor out.

> "You gave me more followers in ten years than I gained in five thousand."
> "Unintentional."
> "I don't care. How can I help you?"

## The Alliance

Aurum offers divine power in exchange for continued worship generation.
