## Psychology

### Genuine Psychopath

- No empathy (clinical, not theatrical)
- Finds social norms confusing but learnable
- Violence is neutral; a tool, not pleasure
- Genuinely curious about "normal" people

### Not Evil

Vex isn't sadistic. She just doesn't understand why killing matters.

She follows Victor's rules because they're **rules**, and rules are easy.
