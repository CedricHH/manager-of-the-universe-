## Hintergrund

### 38 Jahre Guild Leadership

Lysander Corvus kontrolliert die Merchant Guild von Oakhaven seit fast vier Jahrzehnten. Während seiner Amtszeit:

- **Trade Monopoly**: Kontrolliert alle Market-Lizenzen, Shop-Locations, Vendor-Permits in Oakhaven
- **Fee Structure**: Extrahiert 15% "Guild Fees" von allen Market Quarter-Transaktionen
- **Location Control**: Besitzt prime Commercial Real Estate; vermietet zu Premium-Raten
- **License Enforcement**: Unlicensed Merchants face immediate Shutdown und Fines
- **Political Leverage**: Guild Fees finanzieren City Infrastructure → Political Protection

### Pre-Guild Master Era

- Stieg durch Merchant-Ränge auf (Apprentice → Journeyman → Master Trader → Council → Guild Master)
- Baute Vermögen durch Shipping Contracts und Trade Route Optimization
- Bekannt für rücksichtslose Enforcement des Guild Charter

## Psychologie & Persönlichkeit

### Kern-Eigenschaften

- **Bürokratische Präzision**: "Every shop, every stall, every transaction flows through Guild permits" (Ch 104)
- **Legalistic**: Benutzt Guild Charter als Waffe; alles by the book
- **Revenue-Focused**: Betrachtet Market Quarter als persönliche Taxation Zone
- **Methodical Enforcer**: Verhandelt nicht; enforct Rules ohne Exception
- **Not Corrupt**: Glaubt tatsächlich an Guild System (macht ihn gefährlicher)

### Operating Philosophy

- **Control Through Licensing**: No Permit = No Business
- **Market Gatekeeping**: Guild entscheidet, wer Prime Locations bekommt
- **Fee Extraction**: 15% aller Transaktionen fließen zu Guild
- **Legal Warfare**: Benutzt City Regulations um non-compliant Merchants zu crushing

### Motivation

Maintain Guild-System. Protect 38-Jahre-Investment. Prevent Chaos (unlicensed Commerce = Anarchie in seinem Weltbild).

## Strategische Einschätzung

### Lysanders Vorteile:

1. **Legal Authority**: 38 Jahre Guild Charter Precedent
2. **Political Support**: City relies on Guild Fees (18% of Budget)
3. **Market Control**: Kann Permits verweigern, Premium Locations
4. **Enforcement**: Guild Inspectors backed by City Guard
5. **Economic Leverage**: Controls Shipping, Warehousing, Vendor Access

### Lysanders Schwächen:

1. **Dungeon Loophole**: Victors Shop technically on Dungeon Property (Crown Authority)
2. **Public Opinion**: 15% Guild Fees unpopular mit common Merchants
3. **Inflexibility**: Rigid adherence to Rules = poor Adaptation
4. **Voros Connection**: Major Guild Member partnered mit Victor in Arc 1
5. **Revenue Dependency**: City won't support Guild falls Fees drop significantly

### Victor vs. Lysander (Comparative):

| Factor | Lysander | Victor |
|--------|----------|--------|

## Kapitel-Geschichte

### Arc 2: Der Guild War (Kapitel 104-109)

**Kapitel 104 - The Harvest (First Appearance)**
- **Report Received**: Guild Inspector reports unlicensed Apothecary (Insolvia) im Market Quarter
- **Lysander's Analysis**: "An unlicensed shop. In my Market Quarter. Undercutting Guild-approved pricing by 80%."
- **Immediate Response**:
  - Summons Guild Council (Strategic Meeting)
  - Orders Investigation (Chief Inspector Greymark)
  - Legal Review: Can Guild Charter enforce Licensing on Dungeon-sourced Goods?
- **Council Meeting**: "Then we make the Crown choose: Guild revenue or Victor Kaine."
- **Greymark's Mission**: Formal Licensing Demand + Fee Assessment

**Kapitel 105 - The Documentation (Phase 2: Political Escalation)**
- **Day 2**: Victor's Sales doubled (1.247 Customers, 28% Market Penetration); Guild Vendors down 34%
- **Greymark Report**: Zero Violations, 99.7% Purity
- **Lysander Pivot**: Phase 2 (Political Warfare) nach Phase 1 Surveillance Failure
- **Three-Part Strategy**:
  - City Council Petition
  - Vendor Coalition
  - Crown Petition
- **Timeline Acceleration**: Emergency Council Hearing moved von 3 Tage → 12 Stunden
- **"Unlicensed Commerce Prohibition Act"** drafted (57% passage probability)

**Kapitel 106 - The Council (Political Defeat)**
- **Lysander's Position**: Institutional Stability Argument
- **Victor's Counter**: Foundational Law + Economic Data + Voter Pressure (340 Adventurers)
- **Vote**: 4-3 VICTORY für Victor (Motion FAILED)
  - Vex + Dren flipped
  - Sterling swing vote
- **Lysander Warning**: "Phase 3 in 48h" = Extra-legal Methods (Legal/Political exhausted)
- ARMI: 94% Escalation, unknown tactics

**Kapitel 107 - The Sabotage (Phase 3: Supply Chain Blacklist)**
- **Coordinated Refusal**: All 7 major Reagent Suppliers blacklist Victor (100% coordination, 38 Jahre Relationships weaponized)
- **Emergency Guild Assembly**: First in 11 Jahren
- **Agenda**: "Coordinated Response to Unlicensed Dungeon Commerce"
- **Vote Result**: 47 yes, 3 abstain, 2 no → **Total Economic Embargo** approved
- **Scope Expansion**: Reagents, Metals, Equipment, Food—comprehensive Infrastructure cutoff
- **Strategy**: Economic Siege um Victor's Bankruptcy oder Guild Membership zu ezwingen within 2 Wochen
- **Lysander's Assumption**: Victor operates like traditional Merchant (dependent on external Supply Chains)
- **Strategic Error**: Doesn't account für Dungeon Production Infrastructure

**Kapitel 108 - The Substitution (Phase 3 Defeat)**
- **Intelligence Report**: Victor's Apothecary operational trotz total Embargo
- **Discovery**: Victor achieved Internal Production (Dungeon-synthesized Reagents, 96% Efficacy) + External Procurement (Westhold Suppliers, non-Guild)
- **Guild Impact**: 40% Sales Decline für Guild-licensed Vendors
- **Lysander's Recognition**: "He's not operating like a merchant. He's operating like a manufacturer."
- **Council Assessment**: All Three Phases Failed (Legal → Political → Economic Warfare all countered within 1 Week)
- **Economic Projection**: 
  - Continued Fighting = 40% Revenue Loss (2 Wochen)
  - Member Bankruptcies (4 Wochen)
  - Charter Dissolution (6 Wochen)
- **Victor's Negotiation Offer via Valerius**:
  - Guild Integration into "Oakhaven Commerce Authority"
  - Terms: 15%→5% Fees (18-month Phased Reduction), Charter Restructuring, 36-month Transition
  - Lysander = Director of Regulatory Compliance (Advisory Role, retained Authority)
  - Member Protection: No forced Departures, Pension Obligations honored
  - Alternative: 6-Wochen Bankruptcy → Liquidation → Victor acquires Assets at Pennies
- **Lysander's Realization**: "We're middlemen to a system that no longer requires middlemen"
- **Council Vote**: Authorized Negotiation Approach
- **Timeline**: 48 Stunden to respond

**Kapitel 109 - The Negotiation (Professional Resolution)**
- **Guild Internal Conflict**: Hard-liner Faction (Thane + 3) demand Rejection; Pragmatists (4 Councilors) authorize Negotiation (4-3 Vote)
- **Professional Meeting** (Victor + Valerius vs Lysander + Greymark, noon, Guild Hall Private Chamber)
- **Lysander's 3 Data-Driven Concerns**: Vendor Protection, Timeline Viability, Authority Preservation
- **Modified Terms Agreed**:
  - Fee Reduction 24 Monate (extended von 18, prevents Vendor Bankruptcies)
  - Lysander = **Vice Director, Oakhaven Commerce Authority** (Operational Authority vs Advisory, Dignity preserved)
  - Potion Vendor Protection: Employment at 150% Income OR 12-month Transition Fund (9k GP additional Cost)
- **Total Cost**: 95k GP (increased von 87k)
- **Agreement Confirmed**: Professional Handshake, Council Vote scheduled next Day (projected 7-4 Approval)
- **Victor's Philosophy**: "Crushing generates inefficiency. Integration generates value."
