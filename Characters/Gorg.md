## Personality

Gorg is not a mindless beast. He is a retired champion of the Monster Pits who understands "Kayfabe" (Wrestling logic). He treats dungeon defense as performance art.

### Quotes
> "Gorg hold back? Gorg creates illusion of danger?"
> "Gorg wanted to be actor. But world only see monster."

---

## Combat Style (The Showman)
- **Telegraphed Attacks**: Winds up big punches so adventurers can dodge (makes them feel cool).
- **Near Misses**: Throws trees that land *just* next to the healer.
- **The Heel**: Roars at the crowd to generate heat.
