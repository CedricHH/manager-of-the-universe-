## Hintergrund

### Recruitment
- Recruited personally by Victor from Wharton Business School.
- Mentored through her first 5 years in the industry.

### The Betrayal (Chapter 8)
- Delivered the confession folder to Victor during his corporate sacrifice.
- Justified the betrayal with utilitarian calculus: "One executive for ten thousand jobs."
- Was offered Victor's job as compensation.
