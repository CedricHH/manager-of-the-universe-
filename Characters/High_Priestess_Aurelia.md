## Hintergrund

### Church Crisis: Declining Miracle Production

- **Historical Success Rate**: 90% (decades ago)
- **Current Success Rate**: 65% (catastrophic decline)
- **Cause**: Divine Mana Depletion, unknown theological Crisis

Dieser Decline schuf Desperation—Victors Galactic Exchange Miracle Brokerage wurde **strategically critical** für Church Operations.

### Internal Church Rivalry

Aurelia kompetiert mit **Archbishop of Westmarch** für continental Influence. Ihr Miracle Production Shortfall gibt ihm Leverage. Victors Brokerage Deal war as much about **internal Church Politics** as Economics.

### Character Evolution

**Originally**: Zealot Antagonist (Ch 59-61 outline)

## Kapitel-Geschichte (Kompakt)

**Kapitel 91-95 - Coalition Crisis**: Church Paladins detained Victor für "unauthorized Miracle Trading"; Aurelia sah Opportunity; Partnership Negotiations began

**Kapitel 96 - The Cathedral**: 
- Initial Proposal: 50/50 Partnership (Victor supplies, Church distributes)
- Victor delivers Miracles wholesale, Church resells at Markup
- Revenue Split 50/50 (90k GP/month to Victor)

**Kapitel 97 - The Restructure**:
- **Problem**: G.A.C. flagged 50/50 als "dependency relationship" Violation
- **Solution**: Brokerage Model (15% Commission)
  - Victor facilitates Vendor Introductions
  - Church purchases directly von Exchange Vendors
  - Victor earns 15% Facilitation Commission (27k GP/month)
  - **Church retains operational Independence** (no Dependency)
- **Aurelia's Acceptance**: "Fifteen percent of something sustainable beats zero miracles and political humiliation."

**Final Status**: Active Church Partnership (15% Commission auf ~180k GP/month Transactions = 27k GP to Victor)
